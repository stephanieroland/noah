Designed for your household, our kit holds a Go Bag filled with the essentials you need to survive for 72 hours on the move, plus additional food and water for 72 hours of shelter in place survival.

Air Tight Tactical Backpack

We pack 72 hours of survival supplies, including food and water and all the items listed below, into an air tight tactical backapack. The only items below that do not go in the bag are an additional 72 hours of food and water for sheltering in place.

Drinking Water
(1 Serving / Person / 4 Hours)

Packaged water that is shelf stable for 5 years. Each packet of individually sealed water represents one serving.

Meals Ready to Eat
(1 Serving / Person / 7 Hours)

We include meals that can be consummed with and without hot water. Shelf stable for 5 years. Each meal represents two servings.

First-Aid Kit

Approved by the United States Coast Guard, the first-aid kit included in the Noah Kit Go Bag is the top of the line and includes bandages and soutures required to mend basic wounds.

Emergency Blanket

Lightweight and easily folded for ease of storage and transport, emergency blankets break wind, keep people dry and and provide insulation that will keep people sufficiently warm even in below freezing temperatures.

Emergency Radio & Power Station

A source of power in a disaster scenario must be able to function in all conditions and at night. This station works on a hand crank (rather than solar).

Fire Starting Kit

Small but effective. Rubbing the stick against the metallic edge creates sparks for starting fires.

Multi-tool Knife

We ship the classic Leatherman as part of the Noah Kit. It is reliable and effective and has the appropriate variety of tools.

Flashlight

25 meters range and three brightness levels. Rechargeable battery lasts 50 hours per charge. Heavy duty casing to ensure durability.
